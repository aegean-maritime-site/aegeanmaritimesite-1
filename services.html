Our Services

We provide a range of classification and statutory services designed to support compliance with international conventions and flag‑state requirements.

Statutory surveys & certification

Surveys and certification under SOLAS, MARPOL, Load Line, and related instruments, in line with flag‑state instructions.

Classification & Technical Appraisal

Independent assessment of hull,machinery, and safety systems based on recognized technical standards and rule compliance.

ISM, ISPS, and MLC audits

Verification of safety management systems, ship security plans, and seafarer welfare in accordance with applicable codes and conventions.

Plan review and technical assessment

Review of plans and documentation to support safe design, construction, and modification of vessels.

Flag‑state advisory

Guidance on statutory requirements, survey regimes, and documentation to support owners and managers in meeting flag‑state expectations.

Condition Assessment & Special Surveys

Objective evaluation of vessel condition,structural integrity,and machinery performance for life cycle and compliance assurance
