Quality & Compliance

Aegean Maritime Services operates under a structured Quality Management System aligned with IMO instruments, the RO Code, and ISO‑based quality principles to ensure consistent, impartial, and technically reliable service delivery.

Quality management framework

Documented procedures covering survey execution, reporting, documentation control, internal audits, and corrective actions.

Technical governance

Defined responsibilities, competence criteria, and oversight mechanisms to maintain technical integrity across all delegated functions.

Continuous improvement

Regular performance monitoring, feedback, and review to enhance the effectiveness and reliability of our services.
